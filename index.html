---

Overview

I built a Python-based trading bot that analyzes the price of Solana and generates buy and sell signals.

The goal was to better understand how systematic trading strategies work in practice.

How it works

  • Looks at price trends
  • Uses simple indicators (moving averages and momentum) test
  • Generates buy / sell signals
  • Tracks capital evolution over time
---

Key information

  • Asset: Solana (SOL)
  • Market: Spot
  • Timeframe: 4 hours
  • Data: ~7 years of historical data
  • Execution: Paper trading (no real money)
  • Runs continuously on a VPS
---

Results (Backtest)

  • Total return: +341%
  • Average annual return: ~30%
  • Drawdown: -57%
---

Live tracking

The bot is currently running and records its decisions and capital in a CSV file.

This allows me to track its behavior in real market conditions.
