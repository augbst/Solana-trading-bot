Solana Spot Trading Bot (Python)

Python-based systematic trading bot for Solana spot markets, using trend-following and momentum signals on 4-hour candles. The system is deployed on a VPS and tracked live via a CSV log of capital and trade decisions.

Key Facts

  • Asset: SOLUSDT (Spot)
  • Strategy: Trend-following + Momentum
  • Timeframe: 4h
  • Data: ~7 years of historical Binance data
  • Deployment: VPS (24/7)
  • Execution: Paper trading
  • Live tracking: CSV log (capital + trades)

Strategy Overview

The strategy combines:

  • Trend filter: EMA crossover
  • Momentum confirmation: RSI filter
  • Entry: when trend and momentum align
  • Exit: when momentum weakens
